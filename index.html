Frequently Asked Questions

What is PIPS Pro? +
PIPS Pro (Property Inspection Profit System) is a professional mobile-first web application for property investors and inspectors to conduct systematic, data-driven property assessments using 38 specialized protocols with a quantifiable 0-10 rating system.
How does the rating system work? +
Each protocol contains multiple dimensions with specific assessment items. You rate each item on a 0-10 scale. The system automatically calculates dimension scores and overall assessment scores using tier-based weighting (Tier 1 protocols weighted 2.0x down to Tier 5 at 0.5x).
Can I try PIPS Pro before purchasing? +
Yes! The Freemium tier is completely free and includes access to 3 protocols with 3 assessments per month. This allows you to test the complete workflow including photo annotation and PDF report generation before upgrading.
What devices does PIPS Pro work on? +
PIPS Pro is a mobile-first web application that works on any device with a modern web browser (smartphones, tablets, laptops, desktops). No app store download required - simply access via your browser.
What's included in the white-label feature? +
Business tier subscribers can customise PDF reports with their own logo, company colours, and branding. This allows inspection businesses to deliver professional reports under their own brand identity.
How does team collaboration work? +
Business tier includes team features allowing you to invite team members, share assessment access, and collaborate on inspections. Perfect for larger inspection operations with multiple inspectors.
